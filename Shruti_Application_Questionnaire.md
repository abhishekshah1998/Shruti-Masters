## Part 3 — Your Research

**9\. What was your mandatory BPT research project about?** *Topic, what you studied, what you found, and whether it was submitted/approved.*

**Your answer:**  
My internship research project was an experimental study on immediate effects of Lumbo pelvic rotational exercises and hamstring flexibility and running velocity in recreational runners  
In which I studied the immediate effect of these exercises on Hamston flexibility and how they influence running it velocity using various different outcomes and  
The inter-relationship between hamstring flexibility and running it velocity and how it changes after the lumber rotational exercises  
It was found that while my control group showed marginal reduction in the scores  
In theexperimental group highly significant improvements world observed following the Lambo pelvic intervention  
These results indicated that the experimental intervention produced a much more pronounced change in both flexibility and velocity compared to the control group  
After carrying out between group comparison, which focused on the post intervention outcomes, a highly significant difference was identified in the running gate velocity of the experimental group compared to the control group, indicating superiority of specific spinal mobilisation protocol in improving functional speed

My foville  syndrome case study was completed and submitted. It was even presented at a national conference, where I secured first position in undergraduate paper presentation in neuro category
